About

SkillsUSA is a Career and Technical Student Organization (CTSO) that focuses on ensuring its members are ready for the world of work. The organization was founded in 1965 and since then, it has served over 11.2 million members. The SkillsUSA mission is to create leaders and role model American citizens. With 99 available competitions, SkillsUSA is an organization that has something for everyone, ranging from auto competitions to cosmetology.

The Southwest CTA SkillsUSA organization began in the 2012-2013 school year. In that short time, our chapter has accumulated 87 total medals, with 20 of those being gold. Each year our chapter grows and we are excited for many more years of SkillsUSA. In the 2014-2015 school year, there were 100 registered members, making it the largest chapter we’ve had.
